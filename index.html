About Us

The Veterans Meat Locker provides free professionally processed wild and domestic meat for Veterans and their family. Montana Veterans Meat Locker is comprised solely of volunteers, which allows all monetary contributions to be used to cover meat processing expenses.
We rely on animal and cash donations to continue with this amazing opportunity to give back to all those who gave so much for us.

How We Operate

The meat is given free to ANY and ALL veterans, widow and widowers, and Gold Star Families who contact the Montana Veterans Meat Locker. Valid proof of service or VA card is required. Each Veteran is given several pounds and can receive the meat once a month. In 5 1/2 years we have given out 1000,000 pounds to 7000 Veterans all accross Montana. Montana Meat Locker receives wild and domestic animals that are donated by hunters and ranchers with the intention of providing quality food for veterans. The meat is professionally processed and distributed throughout Montana.
